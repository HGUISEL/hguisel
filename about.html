About us

About Us

ISEl
ISEL, which means that Intelligent Software Engineering Lab. It is a laboratory under Professor JC and our research started in 2018. Our mission is to help developers, and our vision is to develop Defect-free Software.

The more complex, the more detect-prone
Our motivation of research is start with this question. "Can we identify defect-prone entities (source code file, binary, module, change,...) in advance?" So, our ground assumption is 'the more complex, the more detect-prone'.

Projects :
Just-in-time Defect Prediction/ Practical Bug Detector + Any Applications for Software Quality Assurance Based on Repository Data Analysis
